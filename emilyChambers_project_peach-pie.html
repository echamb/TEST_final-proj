annie's peach pie

tkc home

desserts

ingredients:
  • 1 (15 ounce) package pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 5 cups sliced peeled peaches
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
instructions:
  • preheat the oven to 450 degrees f.
  • line the bottom and sides of a 9 inch pie plate with a pie crusts.
  • brush with some of the beaten egg.
  • place the sliced peaches in a large bowl, sprinkle with lemon juice and mix gently.
  • in a separate bowl, mix together the flour, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt.
  • pour over peaches, and mix gently.
  • pour into pie crust, and dot with butter.
  • cover with the other pie crust, and fold edges under.
  • flute the edges to seal or press the edges with the tines of a fork dipped in egg.
  • brush the remaining egg over the top crust and cut several slits in the top crust to vent.
  • bake for 10 minutes in the preheated oven, then reduce the heat to 350 degrees F and bake for an additional 30 to 35 minutes, until the crust is brown.
  • cool until warm, serve and enjoy!
