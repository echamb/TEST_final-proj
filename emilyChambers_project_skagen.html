appetizers

ingredients:
  • 1 lb. pre-cooked or steamed shrimp, finely chopped
  • 5 tbs mayo
  • 1 handful of chives, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp cayenne
  • 1 head of bibb or butter lettuce
  • 1/2 stick butter for toast
  • 1 loaf white bread, sliced diagonally into triangles, crust removed
instructions:
  • mix all ingredients except lettuce, butter and bread together and store in fridge
  • in a frying pan, melt butter and fry slices of bread until golden brown
  • break lettuce into small pieces, about 1"-2", and layer on top of fried bread slices
  • scoop 1 tbs of shrimp mixture ontop of lettuce
  • serve and enjoy!
