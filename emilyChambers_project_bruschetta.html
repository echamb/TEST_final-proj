appetizers

ingredients:
  • 20 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 large sweet onion
  • 2 colves of garlic
  • 1 small fresno chili
  • 1 large handful of basil
  • goat cheese for spreading
  • baguette bread cut on a slant into 1/2" slices
instructions:
  • coarsley chop all ingredients
  • throw chopped ingredients into a blender and pulse until desired consistency
  • drain over a fine sieve
  • toast bread slices
  • spread goat cheese on toast
  • add a scoop of bruschetta mixture on top of cheese
  • serve and enjoy!
