maryland crab cakes

tkc home

main courses

ingredients:
  • 6 stone wheat thins, pounded into fine powder.
  • 1 tbs coarse mustard
  • 2 lbs maryland crab
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbs worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbs old bay
    • for cooking:
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 2 tbs butter
instructions:
  • combine all ingredients and refridgerate for 1-2 hours.
  • form into eight patties.
  • sprinkle old bay on top of patties.
  • melt butter with canola oil in frying pan.
  • fry crab cakes for about 4 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  • serve and enjoy!
