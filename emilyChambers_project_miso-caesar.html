soups & salads

caesar dressing ingredients:
  • 1/4 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 2 tbs mayo
  • 1 tbs miso paste
  • 3/4 tbs dijon mustard
  • 1/2 garlic clove finely grated
  • 2 tsp honey
  • juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 2 tbs chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • salt & pepper to taste

salad ingredients:
  • 3 cups chopped kale
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1/4 cup shaved pecorino romano cheese
instructions:
  • mix all dressing ingredients together in small bowl
  • in a large bowl, mix together kale with dressing
  • top with pumpkin seeds, granola and pecorino romano
  • serve and enjoy!
