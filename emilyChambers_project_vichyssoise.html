soups & salads

ingredients:
  • 2 cups finely diced raw potatoes
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 6 leeks, cleaned and cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 3 cups chicken bouillon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • a dash of nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 cups sour cream or heavy cream
  • chopped chives
instructions:
    in a large stock pot:
  • melt the butter in the pot and cook the leeks gently, tossing them lightly, for a few minutes.
  • add the thinly sliced potatoes and season lightly with salt & pepper. cook for 12 minutes.
  • add the chicken bouillon and bring to a boil.
  • lower the heat and simmer the leeks and potatoes slightly covered until tender, about 30 minutes.
  • put this mixture in the blender (you will need to blend it in two lots) and blend for 1 minute, or until smooth.
  • chill.
  • when ready to serve, add sour or heavy cream and top with chives, cayenne or other toppings.
