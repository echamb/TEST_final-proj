curried carrots

tkc home

side dishes

ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 c carrots – sliced into 1/2” rings
  • 4 tbs butter
  • 3 tbs brown sugar
  • 2 tsp curry
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne (optional)
instructions:
  • put sliced carrots in saucepan with water to cover.
  • bring to a boil and simmer gently for 15 minutes.
  • drain and return carrots to pan.
  • add butter, brown sugar and curry.
  • can be prepared ahead at this point.
  • cover saucepan and heat under low heat for 10 minutes until butter melts and the sugar slightly caramelizes.
  • serve hot and enjoy!
