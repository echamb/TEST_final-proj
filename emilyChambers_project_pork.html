carolina style pulled pork

tkc home

main courses

ingredients:
  • 1 3-4 lb pork butt
  • 1/2 onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3/4 c apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tbs brown sugar
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne powder (optional)
  • a few shakes of red pepper
bbq sauce:
  • 3/4 c yellow mustard
  • 2 tbs dijon mustard
  • 1 tbs brown sugar
  • 1 tbs honey
  • 1 tbs miso
  • 1 tbs butter
  • pepper
  • chili powder
  • old bay
  • garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
instructions:
  • combine all ingredients for the pork in a crockpot.
  • cook on low for 12 hours.
  • combine ingredients for bbq sauce in a small saucepan and bring to a slow boil.
  • transfer to a container and let sit in the fridge at least 5 hours or until pork is ready.
  • when pork is ready, transfer to a bowl with vegetables and shred with two forks.
  • drizzle 1/4 cup of remaining juice from crockpot over pork.
  • serve with bbq sauce and enjoy!
