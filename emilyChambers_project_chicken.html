soups & salads

ingredients:
  • 1 3-4 lb chicken
  • kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 large yellow onion, thickly sliced
  • 4 carrots cut into 2-inch chunks
  • 1 lemon, cut in half
  • 1 head garlic, cut in half crosswise
  • 4 tbs (1/2 stick) butter, melted
instructions:
    pre-heat oven to 425 degrees f
  • remove chicken from packaging and pat dry with paper towel.
  • sprinkle salt and pepper over the chicken, including the inner cavity.
  • in a cast iron skillet, arrange lemon, garlic, carrots and onions cut-side down.
  • place seasoned chicken on top, tying the legs with twine.
  • pour melted butter over chicken.
  • roast until chicken is brown, about 45 minutes to 1 hour.
  • once complete, remove from oven and let rest for 15 minutes.
  • after chicken has rested, carve, serve and enjoy!
