mom's potato salad

tkc home

side dishes

ingredients:
    for potatoes
  • 12 red skin potatoes- quartered and roasted
  • 1 tbs herbes de provence
  • 1 tbs sea salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbs olive oil
    for salad
  • 6 hard boiled eggs – coarsely chopped
  • 1/3c celery – coarsely chopped
  • 10 -12 Green onions – sliced
    for sauce
  • 1c mayonnaise
  • 3 tbs old bay
  • 2 tbs sugar
  • 3 juice of lemons
instructions:
  • drizzle olive oil on quartered potatoes with spices.
  • roast for 45’ in 375 degree oven (or until lightly brown and firm)
  • let cool.
  • while roasting potatoes, hard boil 6 eggs. bring eggs to a boil. boil for 10’ minutes, turn off heat and let cool. when cool, remove shells and coarsely cut.
  • in a separate bowl, whisk together four ingredients for sauce.
  • combine sauce, cooled potatoes, eggs, celery, and onions gently together.
  • serve and enjoy!
