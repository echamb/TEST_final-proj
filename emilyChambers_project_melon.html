appetizers

ingredients:
  • 1 ripe cantaloupe
  • 10-15 slices prosciutto
  • fresh picked basil
  • salt & pepper
instructions:
  • using a spoon, discard seeds from center of cantaloupe
  • using a melon baller, scoop out melon balls about 1" thick
  • cut prosciutto slices in half
  • wrap each melon ball with 1/2 slice of prosciutto
  • garnish each ball with 1 basil leaf
  • season with salt & pepper to taste
  • serve and enjoy!
